Work

Creating a startup is an exhilarating experience. I know, I've done it, and I've made almost every mistake in the process. But I got enough things right to bootstrap a new business and grow it organically to over $20 million in annual revenue.

If you're passionate about an idea and willing to knock down any obstacle to make your vision a reality, then I will want you to succeed. I can help with sweat, money, and experience.

To elaborate:

Sweat
  • I can build your Minimally Viable Product.
  • I'll iterate through the "Build / Measure / Learn" lean startup cycles until we achieve traction.
  • I have a preference toward Python, but can work in any language.
  • I'm a strong product guy, so I can handle usability issues and work with underspecified requirements.

Money
  • I can provide seed capital.
  • I prefer to make investments where I add value beyond money (i.e., build something, or leverage domain expertise).

Experience
  • Strategically, I can help you identify the single thing you need to focus on that will determine your success or failure.
  • I can help with tons of operational issues, like accounting, finding office space, legal documents, etc.
  • Have you thought much about the culture you want to create before you make your first hire? You should, and I can tell you why.

So if you're looking for help in any of these areas, please be in touch. I'm always happy to meet for coffee, or just answer a random question.
