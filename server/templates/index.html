Projects

Here's a sampling of some recent side projects:
  • Kondit, a conversation platform. Users can request/offer help on various topics, and the site manages a flow between users that lets members call one another without revealing private phone numbers (via Twilio) or engange in live video chats (via TokBox).
  • media_flow.js. A jQuery plugin for creating a continuous flow of elements in a browser. This effect can work well when browsing a large collection of visual data.
  • Whimsi, a high-end fashion e-commerce site that uses media_flow.js for presentation.
  • FaceBook Blaster. See a continuous flow of photos from your FaceBook feed. Uses media_flow.js.
  • Video Wall. Get YouTube's top 1000 videos of all time blasted into your eyeballs.
